Maven provides a Microsoft Teams bot that brings the full Maven experience directly into Microsoft Teams.

## Installation

To install the Microsoft Teams app on your agent:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Microsoft Teams app">
    - Navigate to the **Apps** page in the **Agent Designer**
    - Click on **Browse & Install** on the **Apps Directory** tile
    - Search for "Microsoft Teams" and click on the **Microsoft Teams** app tile
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    - Click on the **Install** button
    - Provide your Microsoft Tenant ID [Learn how to find your Tenant ID](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/entra/fundamentals/how-to-find-tenant)
    - See below for details on configuration options
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Setup within Microsoft Teams

After you install the **Microsoft Teams** app in **Agent Designer**, click on the
**Instructions** tab. Add the Maven app to your Microsoft Teams workspace.

### Configuring in a Channel

1. In a channel, type "@Maven configure". Enter your desired Agent ID and Organization ID that you would like to link your bot to.
2. Type @Maven followed by your question.

Contact support@mavenagi.com for support.
